About Water Privatization

Privatization can lead to higher operating costs
Privatization can increase the cost of financing a water project by 50 to 150 percent
Privatization typically leads to a loss of one in three water jobs
After privatization, water rates increase at about three times the rate of inflation, with an average increase of 18 percent every other year
For the typical household, privately owned water utility service costs 59% more than public water service/div>
